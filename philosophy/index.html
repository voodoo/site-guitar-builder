Philosophy

Design

  • Modern
  • Light
  • Beautiful wood

I want my guitar to be simple, beautiful, light and resonant.

Visually, I wan't to see beautiful wood. No paint job can compete with the abstact figures found in spalted maple or figured walnut or paduk, etc.

One pickup is good enough. In fact I believe multiple pickups can have a negative effect on the tone because of the magnetic effects on the strings, even when the pickup is not engaged electronically.

A tremolo is not worth the overhead in weight or tuning instability.

Build

  • Neck through I believe a neck through is an essential part of the guitar.

    Here is why ...
    • Resonance and stability
    • More control over upper fret access and shape
    • No bolts, or glue, to keep the two halves of the guitar together
  • Floyd Rose Speedloader
    • No traditional tuners on the head
    • No string winding trees
    • No string over the bridge or nut
    • Fine tuners on the bridge for quick, accurate tuning

General

  • Learn from the past
  • Try new things
  • Always be learning
  • Simplify, remove, repeat
