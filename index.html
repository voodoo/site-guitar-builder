What is this?

Visual guitar building tools

Builder
  • A virtual, dynamic, sharable representation of a guitar configuration
  • Inventory of guitar parts necessary for builds
2D tools
  • Pixelmator tools for drawing the (mostly wood) parts
Builder is open source

Use it to build your own builder

Who is this for?

  • Builders
    • Gather the necessary details for the builds
    • Provide your own inventory that represents your opinions
    • Build your own visual representations
    • Visually document your builds - automatically adding content to your site for marketing purposes
  • Players
    • Who want to build and/or design their own guitar
    • Share your ideas with others - have others contribute and/or provide feedback
    • Articulate your design in detail so the builder can bring it to life

Why?

One day I decided I wanted to build my own guitar.

I documented the process , to help the builder, and these tools are the distillation (and extension of) that process.

I hope you find them useful.

Paul Vudmaska, Sharktail guitars

PS

Right now, things are completely static - on purpose, there are great benefits to this.

In the future, Builder may be a sort of portal. Here are some of the use cases.

Builders
  • Use the tools to document your builds in a visually appealling, easy way
  • Be a recommended builder
  • 'Builds' will be a little like a forum thread but more focused on the process
  • Collaborate with the buyer and others
  • Bid on builds
Buyers
  • Have builders bid on your build
  • Provide the highest price possible - allow builders to bid
  • Thereby inverting the buying process
Both
  • Use Builder & Talk (forum) as a community for guitar enthusiasts
  • Links between Talk and Builder will be practical and useful
  • For example, profiles in Talk will have links to their virtual build (or actual build) - identifying what makes them tick in terms of guitars
